Hey, you found us.

Pink Light is a collective for Nashville musicians who want to connect beyond the stage. We host events, curate playlists, and build a space where artists can actually be themselves — no industry pressure, no networking facades, just real people making real music.

This site is our digital clubhouse. Check back here for event announcements, playlist drops, recaps, and whatever else we feel like sharing. Think of it like a bulletin board at your favorite dive bar, except it glows pink and lives on your screen.

If you're a musician in Nashville and you're tired of the same circuit — we're your people. Come find us. Look for the pink light.
